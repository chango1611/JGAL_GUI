Defined Variables of the Gene

Genes that are successfully added are shown in the defined variables. Where you can edit and / or remove them.

To edit a gene simply press it in the list and then press the "edit" button (without the quotes). By doing this, the gene information is displayed for editing. The difference is that when you press add, instead of adding a new gene, the gene that was selected will be edited.

To remove a gene simply press it in the list and then press the "remove" button (without the quotes).
