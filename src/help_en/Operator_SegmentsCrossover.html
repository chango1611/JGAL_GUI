Segments Crossover

The crossover segment is a variant of the Multi-Points crossover, where the number of crossing points may vary. The fixed number of crossings is changed by a chance to change of segment in any gene.

For example, if the probability of switching segment is 0.2, then the chance of finishing a segment in each gene is 0.2 and the expected number of segments will be 0.2 * m, where m is the number of genes in the chromosome.

The segments crossover requires the user to enter the segment change probability.
