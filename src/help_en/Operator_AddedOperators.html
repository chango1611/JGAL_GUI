Added Operators

Operators that are successfully added are shown in the Added Operators. Where you can edit and / or remove them.

To edit an operator simply press it in the list and then press the "edit" button (without the quotes). By doing this, the operator information is displayed for editing. The difference is that when you press add, instead of adding a new operator, the operator will have edited the one that was selected.

To remove an operator simply press it in the list and then press the "remove" button (without the quotes).

It is also possible to remove all the operators by pressing the clean button at the top of this area.
